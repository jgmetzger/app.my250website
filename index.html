Questions
Do I really pay nothing upfront?
Nothing. We build the site, send you the preview, and you pay only once you love it. If you don't — you owe us nothing.
What do I need to provide?
A 5-minute form: venue name, cuisine or drink focus, hours, location, and any photos you have. That's everything. No design decisions, no templates, no tech.
Can I get the site in Spanish too?
The base price covers an English site. If you'd like a Spanish version added with a language toggle, that's an additional €75. Most venues in the tourist areas find English-only works perfectly.
Can I request changes?
You'll see a full preview first. Request any changes you want — we revise within 24 hours, included in the price. Need further tweaks after approval? €25 per revision.
What's the €20/month for?
Hosting, domain renewal, SSL, speed, and support. Need to update your hours, menu, or add a special event? WhatsApp me and I handle it. You never log into anything.
What if I cancel?
Cancel anytime. We transfer the domain to you. You keep your web address no matter what.
Will this help people find us on Google?
Yes. Every site is SEO-tuned for local search — title, structure, meta, all optimised for your venue type and location. A website on your Google Business profile also boosts your Maps visibility, which is where most tourists find you.
